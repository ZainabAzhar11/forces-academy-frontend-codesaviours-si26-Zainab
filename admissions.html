Admissions at Forces Academy

Follow our simple steps to enroll and become part of Pakistan's premier defense-focused educational academy.

Start Application →

How to Apply

Step 1: Fill the Online Form

Provide personal, contact, and academic details in our secure online portal.

Step 2: Upload Documents

Submit transcripts, certificates, CNIC/B-form, and passport-size photos online.

Step 3: Pay the Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee through bank transfer or online payment.

Step 4: Schedule Tests

Book your entrance test or interview slot as per the academy’s instructions.

Step 5: Receive Confirmation

Receive confirmation and next steps via email or student portal.

Eligibility Criteria

FSc Pre-Medical

Minimum 60% in Matric Science.

FSc Pre-Engineering

Minimum 60% in Matric Science & Mathematics.

Matric Arts & Science

Class 8-10 students with good academic performance.

ISSB & Interview Prep

Qualified candidates for defense selection and leadership programs.

Required Documents

  • Academic transcripts
  • National ID / B-Form
  • Passport size photographs
  • Character certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate
